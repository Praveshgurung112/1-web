About Us


MCM DAV College Kangra is a strong link in the chain of DAV institutions that have
come to serve the cause of education and social upliftment not only in our country
but also in many foreign countries like USA, UK, Japan, Mauritius, Nepal etc


After the reorganization of states, a need for an institution of higher education was
felt in this area, as the entire Kangra district had only one Government College.
In 1975 DAV College Kangra was established. It was later renamed as MCM DAV
College in memory of the great jurist from the area -Sh Mehar Chand Mahajan.

The College was established in an area which was then educationally and
economically backward. In the last four decades of service the College can proudly
claim to have substantially changed the education scenario. Our pass outs have

The college has been granted permanent affiliation by HPU Shimla vide letter no.
1/44-1/74-HPU(ACD) dated 23.11.1979 and is also included in the lists prepared
under section 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC ACT 1956.
