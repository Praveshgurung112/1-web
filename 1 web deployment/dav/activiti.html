Activities


Co-Curricular Activities
Various societies and clubs function under a Teacher Incharge of extracurricular activities. The teacher inchrage trains desirous
students in various activities and recommends their names to be sent for inter college competitions.

sports


Sports are an integral part of education for all round development of cherished personality. All sports activities in the college
are organized by Athletic Council appointed by the Principal.

The college sends various teams to participate in H.P. University Inter College Competitions.

National Service Scheme (NSS)


The college has two NSS units, which provides an opportunity to the youth to understand the current problems of the society.
Students learn the sense of service before self, the quality of leadership and discipline as also feeling of involvement in national
reconstruction. The NSS provides for various types of projects like social service camps in rural/urban areas, campus projects,
literacy campaign etc.

Students desirous of joining NSS should fill in the prescribed form at the time of admission

College Magazine “Sugandhi”


The College Magazine “SUGANDHI” comprises of six sections namely Hindi, English. Science, Pahari, Sanskrit, Planning
Forum and NSS. It is published by an Editorial Board consisting of staff members and student editors.
